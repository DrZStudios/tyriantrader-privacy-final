Privacy Policy for Tyrian Trader

Last updated: April 28, 2026

Tyrian Trader ("the App") is developed by DRZ Studios. This privacy policy explains how the App handles information.

Information We Collect

Tyrian Trader does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The App does not require account registration or login.

Data Stored on Your Device

The App stores your watchlist items and price alert preferences locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Guild Wars 2 API — used to retrieve publicly available item and pricing data. No personal data is sent. See ArenaNet's privacy policy at arenanet.com.
  • Guild Wars 2 Wiki — used for item name search. No personal data is sent.
  • Google AdMob — used to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve ads. See Google's privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy.

Internet Access

The App requires internet access solely to retrieve publicly available Guild Wars 2 Trading Post price data and to display advertisements.

Notifications

The App may send push notifications to alert you when item prices meet your configured thresholds. You can disable notifications at any time through your device settings.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact
