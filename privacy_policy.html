Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 1, 2026

This privacy policy applies to all mobile applications published by Dr Z Studios on the Google Play Store, including but not limited to:

  • Tyrian Trader (com.drzstudios.tyriantrader)
  • Tyrian Timer (com.drzstudios.tyriantimer)

Throughout this policy, these are collectively referred to as "the apps."

Information we collect

Dr Z Studios does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII) about you. The apps do not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide any personal data to function.

The apps store the following information locally on your device only:

  • Your boss subscriptions and watchlist items
  • Your notification preferences and active hours settings
  • Your premium purchase status

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to Dr Z Studios or any third party. If you uninstall the app, this data is deleted.

Data shared with third parties

The apps integrate with the following third-party services, which may collect their own data subject to their own privacy policies:

Google AdMob

The free tier of the apps displays banner advertisements served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect a non-personal advertising identifier (Android Advertising ID) to deliver relevant ads. You can reset or opt out of personalized advertising at any time through your device settings (Settings → Google → Ads).

AdMob's privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Google Play Billing

If you choose to purchase the Premium upgrade, the transaction is processed by Google Play Billing. Dr Z Studios receives a confirmation that the purchase succeeded but does not receive your payment information, name, address, or other billing details — those remain with Google.

Google Play's privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Guild Wars 2 API (Tyrian Trader only)

Tyrian Trader retrieves real-time item pricing data from ArenaNet's official public Guild Wars 2 API at https://api.guildwars2.com. This API is publicly accessible and does not require authentication. Tyrian Trader does not send your personal information to this API — only the IDs of the items you have chosen to track.

ArenaNet's privacy policy: https://www.arena.net/en/legal/privacy

Crash and performance reporting

The apps use standard Google Play Console crash reporting, which collects anonymous diagnostic information when the app crashes. This data is used solely to identify and fix bugs and is not used for advertising or any other purpose.

Children

The apps are not directed at children under 13 and do not knowingly collect data from them. If you believe a child has provided data through one of the apps, please contact us and we will work to remove it.

Permissions

The apps request the following Android permissions:

  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS — to send you the boss spawn alerts you've subscribed to (Tyrian Timer) or price alerts you've configured (Tyrian Trader).
  • SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM / USE_EXACT_ALARM — to schedule notifications at the exact spawn moment (Tyrian Timer).
  • RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED — to restore your scheduled alerts after device reboot.
  • REQUEST_IGNORE_BATTERY_OPTIMIZATIONS — to ensure notifications fire reliably on devices with aggressive battery management.
  • INTERNET — to fetch real-time data from the Guild Wars 2 API and to load advertisements.

Your rights

Because Dr Z Studios does not collect or store any personal data, there is nothing to access, correct, or delete on our end. You can clear all locally stored app data at any time by:

  1. Going to your device's Settings → Apps → [App Name]
  2. Tapping Storage → Clear data

Or by uninstalling the app entirely.
